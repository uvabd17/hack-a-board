# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard
Participants are expected to communicate respectfully and constructively.

Examples of acceptable behavior:
- respectful feedback
- collaborative problem-solving
- clear technical discussion

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- harassment or discrimination
- personal attacks
- publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict access for violations.

## Reporting
Report conduct concerns privately to maintainers.
